How To Play


Mode 1 - Quick Match


1 - Toss Time : Choose Your Toss That is Head ot Tail. If you won the toss You Will make The Choice whether to bat or bowl Else the computer Will make the Decision.


2 - Match Starts : If you are Batting , You have to score runs by selecting The runs Buttons from 1 to 6 . If you are bowling , you have to take the wicket of the computer by predicting its choice.


3 - Result : One who scores More then the other wins the Match.



Mode 2 - Hit a HighScore


Here you can keep on scoring the runs unless you lose your wicket to the computer.

You can take the screenshot of your Highscore and send it to the following mail id to make your name and score displayed in the leaderboard.

worldofhandcricket@gmail.com



Rules Of The Game


Out : while you are Batting,If your choice is same as the computer's choice you are out.


Wicket : While you are Bowling,If your choice is same as the computer's then you got a Wicket.
